About Us

Welcome to Prem Dance Studio, a vibrant space dedicated to the art of dance and well-being. Established on June 5, 2024, our studio has quickly become a hub for dance enthusiasts and fitness lovers alike.

Founded by Rohit Tiwari, along with the support of our co-founders Shivanshu Mishra and Saurabh Yadav, we are committed to fostering a community where creativity and physical health are celebrated through movement.

At Prem Dance Studio, we believe that dance is more than just an art form; it is a powerful medium for expression, fitness, and connection. Our studio offers a variety of classes including Zumba, Aerobics, Yoga, and Meditation, each designed to cater to different skill levels and interests. Whether you are looking to improve your fitness, explore new dance styles, or simply find a place to unwind and meditate, we have something for everyone.

Our team of experienced trainers, led by Prem Chopra and Shivam Kumar, is dedicated to helping each participant achieve their personal goals. With a deep passion for dance and a commitment to teaching, our trainers ensure that every class is engaging, fun, and rewarding. Join us at Prem Dance Studio and be part of a community that thrives on rhythm, wellness, and positivity.
